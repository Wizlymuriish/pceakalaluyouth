About Our Youth Department

PCEA Kalalu Youth is a dynamic and God-fearing community of young believers within the Presbyterian Church of East Africa (PCEA) Kalalu Parish. We are a generation deeply committed to walking in the footsteps of Christ, growing spiritually, and building one another in love, truth, and service.

Our fellowship exists not only to nurture faith but to empower youth to discover their God-given purpose and live it out boldly in every area of life.
